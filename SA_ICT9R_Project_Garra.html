Ways To Prevent
Fire

Preventions


Image Preventions Details
 Cooking oil should be kept far away from the stove.  Keep an eye on the stove when cooking with oil.
 Be sure to properly extinguish smoking materials.  Unsafely discarded smoking materials can smolder before catching fire.
 Avoid using outlet extenders or plug-in power bars.  Never try to fit a three-pronged plug into a two-slot outlet.
 Blow out candles before leaving a room or going to bed.  A candle is an open flame, which means that it can easily ignite anything that can burn.
 Store any flammable products correctly.  Always keep them away from potential ignition sources in a ventilated storage bin.
